# RFQ-1000 — Meridian Estate: Master Smart Home Specification

## 1. Programme Context

Master specification for the Meridian Estate ultra-luxury private residence to be constructed at Lot 100, 10 Fry Lane, Forest Range, SA. Consolidates requirements from planning sessions July–September 2025 (internal reference: RFQ-KV-2025-001, compiled 2025-08-21). This is the primary source document for all estate sub-domain RFQs.

**Estate Principal:** Kevin Slade | **Programme:** Meridian | **Location:** Forest Range, Adelaide Hills, SA

---

## 2. Site & Location

- **Lot:** Lot 100, 10 Fry Lane, Forest Range, SA — ~13.9 ha / 34 acres
- **Elevation:** 252–615 m ASL (average ~465 m); rolling hills and elevated plateau
- **Topography:** Undulating plateau — main residence on high ground; slopes enable earth-bermed theatre, wellness, and service wings
- **Zoning:** Productive Rural Landscape (93%), Conservation (6%)
- **Bushfire rating:** BAL-FZ — all construction to meet or exceed AS 3959
- **Distance to hotel:** ~22 km / 22–25 min from Lot 17 Fairview Road, Hahndorf

## 4. HVAC & Environmental

- Underfloor hydronic heating/cooling (Stiebel or Rehau chiller) — zoned manifolds
- Ducted inverter AC supplementary zones
- Zehnder ComfoAir Q600 ERV — CO₂ sensor-triggered cycles (>900 ppm)
- Air quality sensors (CO₂, PM2.5, VOCs, humidity) in every occupied room
- Phase-change material (PCM) in slab and north-facing walls — 15–19% HVAC energy reduction
- Geothermal HVAC loops for wellness suite and theatre
- Greywater recovery (GreySmart 500 L) — toilets and irrigation reuse
- Rainwater: 100,000 L underground tanks (shared with fire suppression)
- Whole-home essential oil diffusion via HVAC; KNX/Josh.ai integration
