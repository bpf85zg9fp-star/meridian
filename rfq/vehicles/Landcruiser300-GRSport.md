# RFQ-1002-002 — Toyota LandCruiser 300 GR Sport

## Significant Change from Prior Batches

**The tow vehicle for the family Australia touring plan (RFQ-1013) has changed from the Bentley Bentayga (specified in Batch 001) to a Toyota LandCruiser 300 GR Sport.** This is a substantive fleet change, not an addition. RFQ-1013 should be updated to reflect LC300 as the tow vehicle once the final trip-cost model is completed.

**Trailer also changed:** Batch 001 referenced "Bruder EXP-8"; Batch 002 consistently references **"Bruder EXP-12"**. This may be a model correction or a further decision change — flagged for Kevin's confirmation.

## 1. Role — Two-Phase Vehicle

1. **Interim daily family vehicle** (Glenunga period, pre-estate-completion)
2. **Long-range touring platform** for the eventual around-Australia expedition, replacing the Bentayga in that role

Recommendation confirmed: LC300 GR Sport suits both phases without compromise — less ostentatious than Sahara ZX for daily/school-run use, while retaining full expedition capability for the later touring phase.

## 4. TPMS (Tyre Pressure Monitoring)

**Recommended: TireMinder Smart TPMS** — native Apple CarPlay support via TireMinder Lite app; supports up to 10 sensors (4 vehicle + 2 trailer + 2 spares + expansion).

**Sensor layout:** 8 total — 4 vehicle (FL/FR/RL/RR) + 2 trailer + 2 spares.  
**Sensor type:** Internal preferred for remote/sand travel (accuracy, protection from debris) over external (easier install, more exposed).  
**Signal booster:** Recommended given trailer length (>5.5m), EXP-12 composite body RF shielding, and need for reliable temperature alerts on corrugations.

## 5. Fuel

**Diesel grade:** Premium diesel (~50 cetane) from Tier-1 suppliers whenever available — BP Ultimate, Ampol/Caltex Vortex Premium, Mobil Synergy+.  
**Standard diesel fallback:** major brands only (BP, Ampol, Shell, Mobil); avoid biodiesel blends above B5; avoid unknown independent rural tanks in extreme heat.  
**Reserve:** carry minimum 120–150 km extra range beyond target fuel stop.
