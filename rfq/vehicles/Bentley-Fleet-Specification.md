## 1. Bentley Continental GT

### Overview
- 6.0L W12 twin-turbo (or 4.0L V8 twin-turbo)
- The definitive ultra-luxury grand tourer
- W12 confirmed as preferred engine for Adelaide Hills roads and long-haul grand touring

### Upgrade Considerations
- **Mulliner personalisation:** Bespoke interior trim, veneers, hand-stitched leather
- **Audio:** Factory Naim for Bentley system (reference-grade factory fit)
- **Wheels:** 22" Mulliner Driving Specification alloys
- **Suspension:** Optional Dynamic Ride (48V electric anti-roll)
- **Paint:** Bespoke Mulliner colour or extended palette selection
- **Estimated full optioned price (AU):** AUD $450,000–$600,000+ (W12 Mulliner)

### Continental GT RFQ Points
- Confirm W12 vs V8 specification
- Full Mulliner bespoke order through Bentley Adelaide or Bentley Sydney
- Factory delivery or Australian delivery via authorised dealer

- Compatible with estate ABB Terra EV chargers
- AU RRP: ~AUD $88,990
