- **Ready-to-wear:** Chanel, Dior, Valentino, The Row
- **Accessories:** Hermès Birkin/Kelly, Chanel Classic, Bottega Veneta Jodie
- **Footwear:** Christian Louboutin, Manolo Blahnik, Jimmy Choo
