## 4. Recreation — Family Activities

### Music & Creative
- Guitar (electric + acoustic setup): Mesa Boogie or Fender Custom Shop amp; PRS Private Stock or Gibson Custom guitar
- Keyboard: Yamaha Clavinova CLP or Roland V-Piano for studio
- Drums: Roland V-Drums (electronic; soundproofed music studio in estate)

### Family Camping & Touring
- Target: annual family camping Australia tour (east coast or outback)
- Kit: MSR Hubba Hubba tents; YETI coolers; BioLite stove systems; Engel fridge; Thule roof systems
- Vehicles: Bentayga on road legs + support 4WD for remote access
- Family camping tour route options: Flinders Ranges (SA), Great Ocean Road (VIC), Kimberley (WA)

### Sports & Fitness
- Home gym in estate: Technogym equipment; Peloton Bike+; LifeFitness treadmill; TRX/functional training station
- Vasper + infrared training overlays (wellness suite adjacent)
