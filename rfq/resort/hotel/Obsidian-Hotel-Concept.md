# RFQ-1001-001 — Obsidian Hotel — Concept Specification

## Investment Context

Adelaide currently lacks a truly world-class boutique or tech-integrated hotel on par with Sydney or Melbourne. A hotel mirroring Meridian Estate smart home standards would stand alone in the market. The Adelaide Hills, Barossa, and McLaren Vale wine regions have booming high-end tourism demand. SA government grants, incentives, and low-interest financing are available for regional tourism infrastructure projects.

**Scale:** 15–30 room ultra-luxury boutique hotel  
**Positioning:** Tier 5 / world-class; tech-integrated; ultra-premium guest experience  
**Site candidate:** Lot 17 Fairview Road, Hahndorf (working assumption; subject to confirmation)

---

## 1. Site & Architecture

- **Footprint:** ~2,500–4,000 m² (site dependent)
- **Construction:** Concrete slab, double brick or SIP; full acoustic/thermal insulation; commercial fire compliance
- **Style:** Matches Meridian Estate aesthetic — PGH Blue Steel Flash / VMZinc Anthra Zinc palette

---

## 2. Public & Shared Facilities

### Lobby & Lounge
- Custom millwork reception desk with Basalte Ellie touchscreen integration
- Hermès or Liaigre furnishings
- Acoustic ceiling treatments
- Ambient scenting (signature hotel fragrance — Aroma360 or Air Aroma custom blend)
- Art curation: rotating Australian and international collection; Lutron Ketra art lighting

### Restaurant (60+ seats)
- Open kitchen with Chef’s Table
- Kalamazoo or Molteni cooking line
- Sub-Zero, Wolf, Miele appliances
- Hermès dinnerware; Forge de Laguiole cutlery; Waterford crystal
- Custom wine cellar with sommelier-grade storage and by-the-glass WineEmotion dispensers
- Private dining room (10–12 seats)

### Bar / Lounge
- WineEmotion dispensers (by-the-glass wine programme)
- Backlit art liquor display
- Cocktail station: ice machine, cold drawers, glass rinser
- Meural Canvas II ambient art displays
- Top-tier spirit curation: Midleton Very Rare, Macallan Sherry Oak, Hennessy Paradis, Clase Azul Ultra

### Pool Pavilion
- Heated pool, mosaic finish, underwater speakers
- Vergola / Renson motorised louvre roof
- Infrared sauna and cold plunge

### Wellness / Spa
- KLAFS treatment rooms; massage; steam; sauna
- Zenoti spa management software

### Listening Lounge (signature feature)
- Focal Utopia headphone listening stations
- Naim or McIntosh amplification
- Vinyl collection + curated playlists
- Private podcast booths
- Tashén art books; Hermès stationery

---

## 3. Guest Rooms (15–30 keys)

- Same tapware and fixture standard as Meridian Estate (Dornbracht, Toto Neorest NX2)
- Valiryo or Dyson Airblade body dryers in all rooms
- KNX/Josh.ai per room: lighting, HVAC, blinds, AV, do-not-disturb
- Basalte Lena in each room
- Smart lock: SALTO Neo Cylinder + KS Cloud (BLE/NFC/PIN; Mews PMS integration)
- Samsung The Frame or LG OLED display per room
- Kaleidescape Strato C player per room (lossless cinema content)
- KEUCO dispensers and Aesop or L’Occitane en Provençe toiletries
- Hermès bathrobes and Society Limonta linen
- Hästens / Savoir / Vispring beds

---

## 4. Hotel Technology Stack

| System | Product | Function |
|---|---|---|
| PMS | Mews (cloud-native) | Guest journey; booking; smart key; billing; checkout; API-rich |
| POS | Lightspeed Restaurant | iPad-based; restaurant/bar/room service; integrates with Mews |
| Spa | Zenoti | Best-in-class for luxury spa/wellness management |
| Smart locks | SALTO Neo Cylinder + KS Cloud | BLE/NFC/PIN; Mews PMS integration; audit trail |
| Access (lobby) | 2N IP Style | Facial recognition; 5 MP camera; KNX; HomeKit |
| Room control | KNX + Josh.ai + Basalte Lena | Lighting, HVAC, AV, blinds — same stack as estate |
| Video | Kaleidescape Strato C (per room) | Lossless premium cinema content |
| Network | Enterprise Wi-Fi 6E; dual WAN; per-room VLAN isolation | Ruckus R770/R780; Juniper/Aruba core |
| AI concierge | Mews + Zenoti + custom AI layer | Pre-loaded guest preferences; automated room adjustment |
| Data backup | Cloud redundancy (Mews cloud-native); local NAS at property | nightly encrypted backups |
| Digital art | Meural Canvas II in rooms and public areas | Rotating art; property promotions |

**Android guest compatibility:** Full — Mews guest app (iOS/Android), web portal, NFC unlock, tap-and-pay (Google Pay), Chromecast in-room casting.
