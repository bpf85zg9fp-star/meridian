## 4. Landscape & Groundskeeping

### Robotic Mowing Fleet

| System | Model | Coverage |
|---|---|---|
| Estate mowing | Husqvarna CEORA robotic tractor | All open grass — entire 34-acre estate |
| Precision APZ mowing | 3× Mammotion Luba AWD 10000X | APZ (50 m buffer), formal lawns, garden belts |
| Bush zone | Ecorobotix ARA autonomous brush robot | Natural bush zones — fuel load reduction |

### Fire Risk & Surveillance

| System | Model | Function |
|---|---|---|
| Drone dock | DJI Dock (in garage service wing) | Launch/recovery for automated patrols |
| Drone | DJI Matrice 30T enterprise thermal | Daily perimeter sweeps; thermal mapping; ember detection |
| Fuel moisture sensors | 30× FireGrid IoT | 8–12 in APZ; 12–20 in bush zones; mesh network → KNX + drone trigger |

### Irrigation
- Fully buried smart dripper system (all lawns, gardens, orchard, plantings)
- Hydrawise + KNX integration; soil pH and moisture sensors
- Rain sensors: auto-suspend on rainfall events

### Outdoor Lighting
- Smart landscape LED (KNX zoned); path and feature lighting
- Basalte Lena outdoor panel at pavilion (covered position)

### Outdoor Digital Signage
- Weatherproof digital display at main gate/entry point
- KNX-controlled; shows estate name, weather, arrival/departure messages

### Lawns
- Sir Walter Buffalo turf (circular front and rear lawns)
- Robot-mower compatible design (no obstacles in mowing paths)
- APZ: 50 m managed cleared buffer; low-fuel native plantings; buried drip irrigation
