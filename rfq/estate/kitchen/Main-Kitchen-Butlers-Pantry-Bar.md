# RFQ-1000-KIT-001 — Main Kitchen, Butler's Pantry & Bar

## 1. Main Kitchen

### Cabinetry
- Matte charcoal lacquer with walnut accents
- Hafele Matrix/Synergy pull-outs throughout; vacuum drawers; undercounter Liebherr freezer
- Walk-in coolroom (dual-entry, Basalte Lena control, integrated defrost sensors, custom LED lighting)
- Walk-in dry pantry (custom cabinetry, Basalte motion lighting, digital labels)

### Cooking
| Appliance | Specification |
|---|---|
| Showpiece range | Molteni or La Cornue Château 165 (custom enamel + brass/steel finish) |
| Ovens | Gaggenau 400 Series — combi-steam, conventional, warming |
| Cooktop | Gaggenau 400 Series induction flex |
| Vacuum | Gaggenau vacuum drawer + Anova Pro sous vide integration |
| Coffee | Gaggenau CM 470 plumbed-in coffee station |

### Refrigeration
| Appliance | Specification |
|---|---|
| Fridge/freezer | Sub-Zero PRO 48 or column fridge/freezer drawers |
| Wine fridge | Sub-Zero IW-30R integrated wine storage |
| Undercounter freezer | Liebherr undercounter (in island) |

### Sinks & Tapware
| Item | Specification |
|---|---|
| Main sink | The Galley Workstation or Franke Mythos Masterpiece |
| Tapware | Dornbracht MEM or CYO collection throughout (replacing Kohler) |
| Instant hot/cold/sparkling | Zip HydroTap Celsius All-In-One |

### Dishwasher
- Miele G7000 Knock2Open (integrated, handleless)

### Smart Kitchen
- FridgeCam + PantryCam AI inventory management system
- KNX/Josh.ai integration — recipe suggestions via inventory; auto-reorder sensors
- Digital labels in pantry (e-ink, auto-updated via inventory system)

### Household Meal Prep Zones
| Zone | Configuration |
|---|---|
| Main prep island | Integrated scales, portion drawers, vacuum sealer, built-in power |
| Senior/dietary zone | Dedicated surface + Gaggenau combi-steam for nutrient-dense meals |
| Young adult zone | Quick-access fridge drawers (independent prep) |
| Kids zone | Kid-safe prep area; anti-scald Dornbracht fixtures |

## 2. Butler's Pantry / Caterer's Kitchen

- Full secondary kitchen concealed from main kitchen
- Miele Pro dishwashers; Sub-Zero cold drawers; combi oven
- Hafele storage systems; commercial-grade appliances
- Service entry for catering events
- Fisher & Paykel DishDrawer (butler's pantry supplementary)
- Estimated: AUD $210,000–$260,000

---

## 3. Bar & Wine Cellar

### Bar
- Sub-Zero undercounter fridge
- True Residential dual-tap kegerator
- Custom mirrored LED liquor wall
- Hermès dinnerware; Waterford crystal; Forge de Laguiole cutlery
- Basalte Ellie scene control panel
- Bartender station: cocktail tools, ice machine (Scotsman Brilliance nugget), glass rinser

### Wine Cellar
- Subterranean spiral glass wine cellar with motorised lid
- Capacity: 2,000–3,000 bottles
- Climate conditioning: EuroCave Royale or La Sommelère Prestige
- Digital inventory: InVintory system with iPad kiosk
- Zero-UV LED shelving
- KNX temperature monitoring and alert integration

### Tableware & Linen
- Hermès dinnerware throughout
- Society Limonta table linens
- Forge de Laguiole cutlery sets
- Waterford Lismore crystal glassware
