# RFQ-1015-001 — Estate Operating Framework (Summary)

## Nature of This Document

Unlike other RFQ-1000+ documents, this is not a procurement specification. ## 1. Structure (Charter v2.0, as drafted in source)

### Section 1 — Purpose
Establishes governing principles, operational rules, and enforcement mechanisms; aims to preserve privacy/order/long-term value, ensure consistency across family/staff/vendors/guests, remove decision ambiguity.

### Section 2 — Scope
Applies to all family members, staff (permanent/temporary), contractors/vendors/advisors, and guests/invitees. Entry to or engagement with the estate constitutes acceptance.

### Section 3 — Foundational Principles
1. **Family Primacy** — family welfare/privacy/continuity override external interests
2. **Discretion & Privacy** — silence and restraint are operational requirements
3. **Standards Over Sentiment** — relationships/privileges contingent on performance and compliance
4. **Systems Over Individuals** — processes govern decisions, not personal discretion

### Additional sections drafted in source (headings only, per the "House Rules" companion document)
- Family comes first (privacy absolute; no photos/stories shared without permission; disagreements handled privately)
- Privacy is non-negotiable (no social media content from the estate; staff discretion is permanent/lifelong)
- Quality over display (nothing exists to impress outsiders; branding avoided; "if an object needs explanation, it does not belong")
- Loyalty is conditional on standards (earned through performance; ends cleanly without drama when standards slip)

### Later additions (v2.0 expansion)
- **Risk & Security Protocols** — threat assessment, security drills, digital security/cyber hygiene, redundant power/water/HVAC/comms
- **Succession & Continuity Planning** — clear decision authority in principal's absence; staff cross-training; centralised SOP/maintenance/contract documentation
- **Estate Culture & Legacy** — values codification, rituals, non-logo identity anchors
- **Environmental & Sustainability Layer** — energy management, water stewardship
