# RFQ-1000-AV-002 — Perlisten vs Focal Speaker Evaluation

## Significant Note

This conversation opens a new speaker evaluation thread that **partially overlaps with two already-established specifications**: Pro Audio Technology (theatre, RFQ-1000-HT-005) and Focal/Naim CI (whole-home, RFQ-1000-AV-001). The source conversation explores Perlisten, additional Dolby Atmos flagship options (Magico, Wilson Audio, Kharma, Ascendo HALO, Wisdom Audio), and ultimately proposes a **hybrid Ascendo HALO + Wisdom Audio** theatre system — a different direction from the previously specified Pro Audio Technology 32-speaker array. **This requires Kevin's decision on which theatre speaker specification is authoritative** — PAT (RFQ-1000-HT-005) or the newer Ascendo/Wisdom hybrid explored here.

## 1. Perlisten vs Focal — General Comparison

| Feature | Perlisten | Focal |
|---|---|---|
| Sound signature | Neutral, ultra-clean, very low distortion | More musical, warmer (Elegia/Utopia lines) |
| Detail/resolution | Extremely high, forensic | Very engaging but less forensic |
| Dynamics | Exceptional, large-room capable | Strong but slightly softer |
| Imaging | Laser-precise, expansive | Excellent, slightly smoother |
| Home theatre fit | Top choice (clarity + transient response) | Very good, more music-centric |

**Recommendation given in source:** Perlisten for main listening / whole-house integrated audio; Focal retained for dedicated music/living spaces wanting warmth.

## 2. Perlisten Product Recommendations by Zone

| Zone | Model |
|---|---|
| Main theatre L/R/C | Perlisten S7t |
| Theatre Atmos overhead | Perlisten A5t / A7t |
| Theatre subwoofers | Perlisten P15-v2 (×2 or ×4) |
| Large living/music hybrid | Perlisten S7t / A6t + subs |
| Alternative warmer option | Focal Sopra No2/No3 + sub |

## 3. Absolute Pinnacle Options Explored (No-Budget-Constraint Scenario)

For an 11.1.10 Dolby Atmos configuration, the following flagship-tier alternatives were surveyed:

| Role | Options discussed |
|---|---|
| Front LCR | JBL Synthesis SCL Series, B&W 800 Series Diamond, Magico M9/Ultimate III, Sonus Faber Aida, Wilson Audio Chronosonic XVX/Alexandria, Focal Grande Utopia EM, Kharma Grand Exquisite |
| Cinema-native (recommended direction) | **Ascendo HALO 10 Pro Active EXT Cardioid** — CEDIA 2025 Best Home Theater Speaker award; 136+ dB SPL headroom; coaxial point-source + cardioid bass array |
| Surrounds/heights (recommended pairing) | **Wisdom Audio** planar magnetic/line-source — smooth dispersion, uniform coverage for multi-row seating |

**Proposed hybrid:** Ascendo HALO for front LCR (cinematic anchor) + Wisdom Audio for surrounds/heights (enveloping ambience) — explicitly engineered as complementary rather than matched-brand.

### Crossover Strategy (as specified for the hybrid system)

| Channel | HPF (to subs) | Rationale |
|---|---|---|
| Front LCR (Ascendo HALO) | 70 Hz, 24 dB/oct Linkwitz-Riley | Preserves mid-bass authority and dialogue chest resonance |
| Surrounds/Wides (Wisdom) | 80 Hz, 24 dB/oct LR | Keeps planar drivers in lowest-distortion zone; matches Dolby cinema reference |
