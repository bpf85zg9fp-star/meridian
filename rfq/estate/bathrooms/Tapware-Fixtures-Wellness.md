# RFQ-1000-BTH-001 — Bathrooms, Tapware & Wellness Suite

## 1. Tapware — Dornbracht Throughout

- **Collection:** Dornbracht MEM or CYO — entire estate (replaces Kohler previously specified)
- **Master ensuites:** Dornbracht Aquamoon ATT — integrated aromatherapy + chromatherapy body spray
- **Powder rooms:** Dornbracht Touchfree IR faucet; Geberit Sigma80 touchless flush plate
- **Dornbracht selection rationale:** Highest-end German precision tapware; confirmed Tier 5; better integration of wellness features than Kohler; available through AU distributors

## 2. Toilets

- **Model:** Toto Neorest NX2 — all ensuites and powder rooms
- Features: heated seat, auto open/close, bidet with remote, self-cleaning glaze, integrated deodoriser
- Preferred over Geberit AquaClean for flush performance and hygiene system

## 3. Body Dryers

| Location | Model |
|---|---|
| Master ensuites | 2× Valiryo Black Edition full-body dryers (per bathroom) |
| Pool pavilion change room | 2× Valiryo Black Edition |
| Guest bathrooms / powder rooms | Dyson Airblade 9kJ (HU03) |

Valiryo Black Edition: full-body air dryer; 23-nozzle tower; no-contact; eliminates towel use; KNX-triggered.

## 4. Sensor Taps & Flush Plates

- **Basin:** KEUCO IXMO IR sensor basin mixer — all ensuites
- **Flush:** Geberit Sigma80 touchless flush plate — non-Toto zones
- **KEUCO dispensers** throughout all bathrooms (soap, shampoo, conditioner)

## 5. Cabinetry & Layout

- Dual vanities flanking central brick divider in master ensuites
- 2 showers per master ensuite; recessed tub niche; pocket powder room
- Basalte Lena mirrored panels integrated at vanity in all bathrooms
- Custom LED wall mirrors (all ensuites)
- Switchable privacy glass in spa zones and master ensuite windows
