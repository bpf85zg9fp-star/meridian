# RFQ-1007-001 — 55m Ultra-Luxury Expedition Yacht

## Brief

Private superyacht for 5 couples (10 guests) plus 8 crew. Timeless modern classic exterior; dark, rich modern interior aligned with Meridian Estate aesthetic. Full expedition readiness (remote/polar-capable). Layout: 4 VIP guest cabins + 1 Owner's duplex suite with private deck and office.

**Overall length:** 55 m | **Beam:** ~10–12 m | **Draft:** ~3–3.5 m | **Gross tonnage:** ~600–700 GT

## 2. Hull, Propulsion & Engineering

- **Hull:** Steel hull + aluminium superstructure; optional ice-class reinforcement for polar/remote operations
- **Propulsion:** Hybrid diesel-electric (Rolls-Royce MTU Hybrid Series 4000 + Danfoss VFDs) for silent cruising, reduced fuel, long range
- **Pod drives:** ABB Azipods for precise manoeuvring and efficiency
- **Stabilisation:** Quantum ZeroSpeed stabilisers + CMC Electric Fins (comfort underway and at anchor)
- **Dynamic positioning:** ABB Dynamic Positioning 2
- **Generators:** Kohler or Northern Lights Hybrid Silent Series
- **Energy storage:** Corvus Orca lithium system integrated with solar for hotel-load silent running
- **Range:** Minimum 3,500+ nm at cruise; full expedition readiness
- **Speed:** 15–18 knots cruising, 20–22 knots max
- **Navigation:** Fully digital bridge — Furuno/Navico/Simrad; redundant autopilot
