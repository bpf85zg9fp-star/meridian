## 1. $20M Portfolio Allocation Framework (September 2025)

Principles discussed:
- **Diversification across asset classes:** equities, fixed income, real estate, alternatives, cash
- **Geographic diversification:** AU domestic + international developed markets + selective emerging
- **Liquidity ladder:** maintain accessible liquidity for estate construction drawdowns
- **Tax structuring:** SMSF, family trust, and direct holdings — requires specialist AU tax advice
- **Real assets:** estate property and hotel as significant illiquid real asset exposure — balance with liquid portfolio

### Indicative Allocation Framework (planning stage only)

| Asset Class | Indicative Range | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Australian equities | 15–25% | ASX200 blue chip + dividend focus |

| Cash / liquidity reserve | 10–15% | Estate construction drawdown buffer |

## 2. Generational Wealth Framework

- Family trust structure for intergenerational asset transfer
- SMSF for retirement and property investment
- Estate planning: will, enduring power of attorney, succession plan
- Philanthropy: donor-advised fund or private ancillary fund (PAF) for charitable giving
