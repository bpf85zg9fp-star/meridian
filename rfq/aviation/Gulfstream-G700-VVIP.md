# RFQ-1008-001 — Gulfstream G700 VVIP Completion

## Brief

Ultra-luxury, high-tech, near-silent global-range private aircraft for 5 couples (10 guests) plus crew. Selected airframe: **Gulfstream G700** — chosen over the G800 for superior cabin length (17.35 m vs 14.27 m) and 5-zone configuration capability, prioritising space over the G800's modest range advantage.

---

## 1. Airframe Selection Rationale

| Criterion | G700 | G800 |
|---|---|---|
| Range (8 pax, M0.85) | ~7,500 nm | 8,000 nm |
| Cabin length | 17.35 m | 14.27 m |
| Cabin zones | Up to 5 | 4 |
| Sleeping berths | Up to 10–11 (5 couples comfortably) | 8–10 max |
| Engines | Rolls-Royce Pearl 700 (×2, 18,250 lbf) | Same |

**Decision:** G700 selected — cabin volume and 5-zone capability outweigh the G800's range advantage for this brief.

## 4. Cabin Acoustics & Vibration Isolation

Factory G700 already sub-50 dB in cruise (~52 dB); further enhancements specified:

- Active structural damping: Lord Corporation + Hutchinson tuned-mass dampers; Thales anti-vibration mounts on floor beams and seat rails
- 3-layer acoustic sandwich (Kevlar honeycomb + Sorbothane gel + foam barrier) under flooring and panels
- Bongiovi Aviation Active Acoustics + Dirac ART spatial DSP — cancels standing low-frequency hum
- Goodrich inflatable acoustic door seals (main and baggage) to prevent whistle at altitude

## 5. Cabin Audio — Audiophile Aviation Grade

**Selected:** Bongiovi Aviation + Dirac Spatial Audio hybrid system

| Component | Specification |
|---|---|
| Processing | Bongiovi DPS 3D soundfield engine with Dirac Virtuo binaural calibration |
| Amplification | Digital Class-D monoblocks, 32-bit DACs per channel, zone-tuned |
| Speakers | Carbon-Kevlar micro-transducers with soft-dome tweeters, integrated flush into panels/bulkheads |
| Atmos | Certified Dolby Atmos up to 7.1.4, optional head-tracking via headrest channels |
| Calibration | Spatial recalibration per passenger seat via microphone array |

**Alternative considered:** ALTO Aviation nVELOP Premium Surround Sound (aviation-native, 7,000+ business aircraft installed base) — retained as fallback option if Bongiovi/Dirac integration proves complex.
